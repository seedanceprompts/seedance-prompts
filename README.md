## Prompt Engineering Guides

Learn how to write better Seedance 2.0 prompts.

- **[Seedance Prompt Skill](https://github.com/songguoxs/seedance-prompt-skill)** ‚Äî The most comprehensive guide: prompt structure, style control, camera movement, timing
- **[Seedance Prompt Guide](https://github.com/rich5000/seedance-prompt-guide)** ‚Äî Best practices for shot types, scene descriptions, and quality tips
- **[Make Prompt Seedance2](https://github.com/liangdabiao/make-prompt-seedance2)** ‚Äî Step-by-step structured templates you can fill in and customize
- **[Awesome AI Video Prompts](https://github.com/geekjourneyx/awesome-ai-video-prompts)** ‚Äî Cross-model prompt engineering guide covering Seedance, Kling, Wan

### Quick Tips

- **Use timecodes** ‚Äî `[00-05s]` style for multi-scene control
- **Specify camera** ‚Äî close-up, wide shot, tracking, dolly, etc.
- **Reference film styles** ‚Äî "Denis Villeneuve style", "Wong Kar-wai aesthetic"
- **Set constraints** ‚Äî "No text, no watermarks, no gore"
- **Keep under 15 seconds** ‚Äî Seedance 2.0 works best with 5-15s clips
- **Describe lighting** ‚Äî "golden hour", "neon rim light", "volumetric fog"

## Contributing

This list is community-maintained. We'd love your help!

### How to Contribute

1. **Add a resource** ‚Äî Found a great Seedance repo, website, or tool? [Open a PR](https://github.com/seedanceprompts/seedance-prompts/pulls)
2. **Add yourself** ‚Äî Are you a Seedance creator? Add your profile to the Creators section
3. **Fix something** ‚Äî Broken links, outdated info, or missing details? PRs welcome
4. **Suggest a category** ‚Äî Have ideas for better organization? [Open an issue](https://github.com/seedanceprompts/seedance-prompts/issues)

### Guidelines

- Resources must be **directly related** to Seedance 2.0 prompts or video generation
- Include a **brief description** of what the resource offers
- Keep entries **factual and neutral** ‚Äî no promotional language
- Maintain **alphabetical order** within each section where applicable
